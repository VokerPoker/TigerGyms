Welcome to Tiger Gym

Tiger Gym is designed to be a community gym where everyone is welcome to train. It is conveniently located at 1507 Pinto Place in Mt Pleasant near the Tuscarora Tap House. The gym features 4000 square feet consisting of strength training equipment, a cardio room, a Women Only training area, yoga and fitness classes, and men and women locker rooms. Fitness Classes are included for members and $10 per class for non members. From the bodybuilder, athlete, power lifter, or beginner, everyone is welcome to train here. Many who have never performed any type of weight training can be intimidated by a large commercial facility. At Tiger Gym we strive to create an environment where everyone is comfortable not only with the equipment and facility but also with other members.
